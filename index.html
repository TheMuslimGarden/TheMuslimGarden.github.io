How you can help
We would appreciate your help a lot! But we are not in need of money. We would like to ask you for your time. Spending your time taking your kids to the masjid joining Quran reading sessions and helping them improve at reading while you are at home may not be easy. But if you do it for the sake of Allah it will become enjoyable. Especially if you are doing it with your friends. And then you will start seeing your work bringing you happiness in this life and the hereafter inshallah.
You and the people you help will become happy inshallah. For more information on how you can help go to the --Help the ummah page--.
